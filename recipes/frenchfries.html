French Fries

Easy french fries recipe that are easy and resturant quality

Little ingredients needed!

Ingredients

  • 3 Russet potatoes
  • Neutral oil of choice oils such as Canola or peanut
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Heat up your oil in a pan preferbly a cast iron enough to fill it half way and temperature reaches 275 F
  2. Cut potatoes into shape of such as wedges
  3. Slowly drop the potatoes into the oil be sure not to over crowd the pot or pan
  4. After around 5-10 minutes, return fries into a trey and pat dry with paper towels besure not to burn your self!
  5. For best results, spread fries in a metal sheet and freeze in the fridge for up to 4 hours but a day for best results
  6. Heat up your pan again and make sure your oil reaches a temperature of 350 F
  7. Return your fries to the hot oil slowly and fry until golden brown and crispy
  8. Take fries off the pot and into a mixing bowl
  9. Season fries with salt and pepper
  10. Enjoy!
